What is Summer Research Finder   

Summer Research Finder is easy to use, yet hard to explain!

Our idea is called "Summer Research Finder (SRF)", which will match students with college professors. Our main inspiration for making this was when we, personally, had to spend long hours emailing professors for summer research opportunities. While emailing hundreds of professors to receive "no's" from many of them because of certain backgrounds that we didn't have. Our software takes into account the distance between the student and the research institution to ensure a student is comfortable in their preferred location.

How to use it?


SRF is an easy to use platform, requiring only a few simple steps!

1) Create an account. Click to "Sign Up" on the home page. Then follow the steps listed. Just fill in your name, email address, and desired password, as well as type in your zip code, as the website will require it to find univerisities around you area.

2) After creating an account, sign in using your email address you typed in and password you created.

3) Once you are logged in , you will see your dashboard. There will be 2 pending items. The application is the most important link that must be completed. Using answers from the application, the software will be able to auto-generate and email that will be sent to professors from colleges near the user's current location.

4) Wait till you get a response

It's that easy!
